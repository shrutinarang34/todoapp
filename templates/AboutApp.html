About TODO APP

Welcome to the TODO App! This application helps you stay organized by managing your daily tasks. It provides complete CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionality to help you keep track of your to-do list effectively.

Features

  1. Create:
    Add new tasks to your to-do list by entering a title and description for each task. You can set a due date and assign a priority level to ensure better organization.
  2. Read:
    View all your tasks in a clean and intuitive interface. Each task will display its title, description, due date, and priority level. You can easily scan through your tasks to see what needs to be done.
  3. Update:
    Modify existing tasks whenever needed. You have the flexibility to edit the title, description, due date, and priority level of any task. Update tasks to reflect changes or new information as your plans evolve.

  5. Delete:

  6. Search:
    Quickly find specific tasks by using the search feature. Enter keywords or phrases related to the task, and the app will display matching results, making it easier to locate specific items in your to-do list.
